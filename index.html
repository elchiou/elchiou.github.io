I am an AI/ML Engineer at GSK.ai, where I work on developing state-of-the-art deep learning methods to advance precision medicine and accelerate drug discovery.

I completed my PhD at University College London (UCL), where I was advised by Iasonas Kokkinos and Eleftheria Panagiotaki. My research focused on domain adaptation for semantic segmentation with deep learning, for both medical and natural images. The proposed methods facilitate knowledge transfer through unsupervised and semi-supervised model adaptation and generalization.

I received my Diploma in Electrical & Computer Engineering from the Technical University of Crete (TUC) and my MSc degree in Biomedical Engineering from the Technical University of Denmark (DTU).

During my PhD, I did a research intereship with Samsung, where I worked on efficient reference-based image super-resolution.
